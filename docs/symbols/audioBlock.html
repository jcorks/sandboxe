Class Detail
audioBlock()
Audio asset object. An audio waveform is defined in sandboxe as a stream of samples, where samples are 2 channels of information representing the amplitude of the waveform at that point. More specifically, samples are 16-bit signed integers. Though, it is important to note that AudioBlocks represent stereo waveforms, where per sample, there exist two datum values, one for each channel. As such, data for the waveform is interleaved to qualify both channels, where the subsequent sample corresponds to the channel opposite of the current one.
